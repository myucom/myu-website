Frequently Asked Questions

This is a brief list of our most frequently asked questions. For further details or support please visit www.myu.co

What is myU?

myU is a mobile app that allows students and educators to connect via sending reminders, creating discussions, all in social network-like experience that is safe, instant and private. The app makes it easy to connect with anyone in the school community, makes classes more interactive, and sharing fun. We envision a better empowered education through technology.

How much does it cost?

$0.00 - available for free in the Apple App Store and Google Play store.

How did the idea come about?

We’ve all faced trouble with communications at school or as students at some point in our life, we thought it’s about time we do something about it. Technology is changing the world and enhancing our experiences, we thought of creating a tool that adds to education and makes it interesting through:
  • Instant Communication
  • Engagement
  • Interactivity
  • And the main ingredient FUN!

What other services are you compatible with?

Our app is currently Twitter, Facebook friendly you can share your profile with colleagues or students and much more!

How can we partner with you?

If you’re an organization looking to push to partner with us all you have to do is visit www.myU.co

I have a technical problem or support issue I need resolved, who do I email?

It’s best to visit www.myU.co for any technical problem.

How does privacy work?

Your engagement on my is completely safe and private.

Who can see my profile?

Only students and professors within your school community are able to see your profile.

Can I share files?

Currently you can share images and voice notes, sharing files will be available on a later stage of development.

Is myU App available on the web?

Good news! We are currently working on a new design for the app, it provides you with a better feel and more functionality through access via web.
